Tell me Y-SIS: Share, Inform, Stimulate

Tell me Y-SIS is a new way for our members to share the insights of their most recent articles and inform the scientific community via our social channels. We aim to disseminate recent scientific achievements of young researchers and stimulate the exchange of ideas.

Fill out the form if you want to tell us about your last paper and share the highlights with the social community!
