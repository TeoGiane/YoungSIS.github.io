Join Y-SIS!

From January 1st 2017 it is possible to join SIS as “socio ordinario”: the fee for young researchers (under 35 years) is fixed at 35 euros. For this reason we invite all of you to subscribe to SIS as “socio ordinario” and then to fill the form available below.

The by-law of ySIS has been modifided accordingly and is now available on our website (see art.3). If you have any question do not hesitate to contact us!
